2. The East Hall is a 7-bay Tang hall.

### Control conditions for Tier 2
- wording must stay close to the source-supported identity/significance layer

- do not jump from "important surviving Tang timber building" to "therefore all key structural claims in the packet are proven"

## Tier 3 — Limited claims (do not use as main packet assertions yet)

### Why Tier 3 must stay limited
